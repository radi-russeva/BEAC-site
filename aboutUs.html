ARCHITECTENBURO BERKEIN

is a Belgian forward thinking office from Aalter and is expanding internationally.

With the knowledge and experience of architect Geert Berkein, the office has grown steadily over the recent years to a multidisciplinary team of more than 50 dynamic employees, including more than forty architects and designers.

The office mainly focuses on the research and creation of quality and stylish residential buildings in open dialogue with the client.

With a strong ideology and a nod to warm minimalism, both small and large-scale projects are conceived always in relation to their environment.

With its experience, the office combines creativity with realism in harmony with a pleasant living environment.

Sustainability, functionality, client related and economic aspects are our fixed values.
